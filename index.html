CENTRAL ENGLISH SCHOOL

Central English School was established in the year -2009 in the lush green environment at Sadaquat Ashram, Patna. The school is recognized for its commitment towards excellence. This is an English medium school based on CBSE curriculum. At CES, students receive so much more than an ordinary education. We pride ourselves on creating a unique, exciting environment that embraces all types of learners and promotes growth both academically and personally. Students and families are also welcomed into a dynamic and supportive community. Interested in learning more about Central English School? We invite you to keep exploring our website, and get in touch with questions about our values, academics, community and more. Join us today! .

Our Mission

At Central English School, we are committed to creating an educational atmosphere that makes coming here an exciting experience, each and every day. Admission

Central English School is welcoming interested applications for coming sessions, admission start from Nursery to Class- VIII . It is subject to vacancies, Admission is granted on academic grounds irrespective of caste, creed and community. Students are selected through the results of the admission test that they have to undergo upon registration with the school. Fees are to be paid in “quarterly basis”.
